JavaScript Exercises

1. Number Functions

Functions for squaring, halving numbers, calculating percentage, and area of circles.

2. MixUp

Swaps the first two characters of two strings and concatenates them.

3. FixStart

Replaces all occurrences of the first character with * (except the first character itself).

4. Verbing

Adds 'ing' or 'ly' to the end of words based on specific rules.

5. Not Bad

Replaces "not...bad" with "good" in sentences.

6. The Pluralizer

Pluralizes nouns based on a number, handling irregular plurals.

7. The Array

Displays choices with proper ordinal suffixes (1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.).

8. Word Guessing Game

A word guessing game with Wheel of Fortune and Hangman features.

9. The Recipe Card

Creates and displays recipe information including title, servings, and ingredients.

10. The Reading List

Tracks books you've read and want to read.

11. Movie Database

Stores and displays movie information including title, duration, and stars.

12. Cash Register

Calculates the total price of items in a shopping cart.

13. Credit Card Validation

Validates credit card numbers based on specific rules.
